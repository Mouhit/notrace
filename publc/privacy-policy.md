# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address
- Full name
- Password (hashed)

### Usage Data
- Number of secrets created
- Message metadata (not content)
- Device and browser information
- IP address

### Payment Information
- Processed securely through Razorpay
- We never store complete card numbers
- Only transaction IDs and amounts logged

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- Provide the Service
- Send billing and account notifications
- Improve and maintain the Service
- Prevent fraud and abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Data Security

- AES-256-GCM encryption for all messages
- HTTPS for all communications
- Secure authentication with hashed passwords
- Regular security audits

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account Info | Until deletion |
| Payment Records | 7 years (tax requirement) |
| Audit Logs | 1 year |
| Usage Stats | 90 days |
| Deleted Messages | Deleted immediately |

## 5. Third-Party Services

We use:
- **Razorpay:** Payment processing
- **Supabase:** Database hosting
- **Vercel:** Application hosting

Each processes data per their privacy policies.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Download your data
- Delete your account and data
- Opt-out of communications
- File complaints with authorities

## 7. GDPR Compliance

For EU residents:
- Your data is processed lawfully
- You can request data deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
- You can port your data
- We maintain Data Processing Agreements

## 8. CCPA Compliance

For California residents:
- You can request what data we collect
- You can request deletion
- You can opt-out of data sales (we don't sell data)

## 9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for:
- Authentication
- Session management
- Analytics (optional)

You can disable cookies in your browser.

## 10. Children's Privacy

NoTrace is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 11. International Data Transfer

Your data may be transferred internationally for processing. We ensure adequate safeguards.

## 12. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use means acceptance.
